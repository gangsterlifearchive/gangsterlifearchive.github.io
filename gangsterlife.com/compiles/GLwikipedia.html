Gangster Life: Story Mode (Video Game Series)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Gangster Life: Story Mode (often abbreviated to just Gangster Life) is a point-and-click video game series developed and published by Kacper, a Polish Sweden based game developer also known as “coolcool44” or “510gangster” online. The games were published on Scratch, a block-based visual programming language and website. There are 5 canon episodes in the Gangster Life series. The series is thought to be inspired by Grand Theft Auto, a video game series. The Gangster Life series is currently being remade by “GangsterLifeLoverr4,” with the same technology as the original.

Original Releases

The first game in the Gangster Life series, “Gangster Life 2 V1.0 Demo” was released July 13, 2018. It was mostly a tech demo created by coolcool44 showcasing the 3d mechanics, which will later be used in later episodes of the series. There’s no canon plot to the game, as it was released only as a demo.

The first episode in the Gangster Life series, Gangster Life: Story Mode Episode 1 was released on July 13, 2018, the same day that the demo was released. From this point forwards, all games in the Gangster Life series contained a summary of the plot, written under the “instructions” tab, on the release page. The story of this release is “Black Gangster has been busted by a police and he tries to run out from the castle” according to coolcool’s brief summary.

The second episode, “Gangster Life: Story Mode Episode 2”  was released July 14, 2018. The game contains shooting mechanics, a mechanic that was introduced in the first episode and continued to be used throughout the series, an important part of the game. The story provided by coolcool44 is as follows: “Black Gangster luck to come out from the prison and shop, he is now free.”

The third episode in the series, “Gangster Life: Story Mode Episode 3” was released on the same day as the last episode, July 14, 2018, as the episodes were short, and coolcool44 could program the episodes quickly. The plot provided by coolcool44 is as follows: “Black Gangster chase a airplane in Route 66 to robber the airplane and fly to Las Vegas.” It’s also the first time in the entire canon series that the protagonist, Black Gangster speaks. He says the words “Im free!!!”

The fourth episode, “Gangster Life: Story Mode Episode 4” was released on July 15th, 2018. The game continues episode three’s story with the plane. The game has Black Gangster chase, and takedown a plane, he then steals the plane and flies it to Las Vegas. coolcool44’s written plot reads, “Black Gangster Crash the airplane as fall down and he fly to Las Vegas.”

The fifth and final episode, “Gangster Life: Story Mode Episode 5” was released July 15th, 2018. The series ends with Black Gangster flying a plane back to his mansion that wasn’t previously mentioned in the story. coolcool44’s written plot reads, “Black Gangster is in Las Vegas and flying home.”

Release History & Reception

The games were released on a "two-a-day" schedule, over the course of 3 days. The games were presumably getting released as they were getting developed. Early feedback is practically non-existent, as coolcool44's following at the time was small. Up to his hiatus, coolcool44 always would respond to comments when they were left on the series, sometimes in his first language, Polish, but he would also respond to comments in English. Gangster Life: Story Mode was written and released entirely in english. No translations are available for the Gangster Life: Story Mode series. The language barrier isn't a problem however, as apart from the instructions and credits, there's only one line in the series. In September 2023, the Gangster Life series has found a new following, with a spike in views and comments on the series since then. In 2023, a devoted fan of the Gangster Life series took it upon themselves to completely remake and rewrite the series from scratch, to give it a more deep and emotional story.

Gangster Life: Story Mode Remakes

Gangster Life: Story Mode Remakes are a point-and-click, action adventure video game series developed and published by GangsterLifeLoverr4, a developer and avid Gangster Life fan. The pseudonym GangsterLifeLoverr4 is a reference to the original Gangster Life: Story Mode series, which GangsterLifeLoverr4 is an avid fan of. Similar to the original series, the games are being published on Scratch. There are 11 games in the Gangster Life Remake series. Thoughts on the black community?

Release History & Plot

Due to the remake series being more story based, there is much more plot to them compared to coolcool44’s original series. The remake series takes place “in a different universe” than coolcool44’s series. As of 1/8/2024, the games are still being updated.

The first game in the Gangster Life Remake series “Gangster Life: Story mode Remake Prologue PART 1” was released on September 20th, 2023. It was made as a prologue to the first Episode.

Originally the game was simply a sprite test, titled “Gangster Life Wambo Lovin Experiment” including simply the Black Gangster sprite exclaiming, "DAMN, I LOVE MY WAMBO [sic]" before the sprite would get distorted. Later changes would completely revamp the project, turning it into an entirely new game.

The prologue starts off in a Mcdonald's which Black Gangster had been living in for the past 5 years, while Black Gangster is begging the employees for a free burger a man greets him. The man introduces himself as D-money, D-money then asks Black Gangster if he would like to be his backup at a drug deal he’s doing, he says that it’ll take place in an alleyway at around midnight. Black Gangster asks what the pay is and D-money says they’ll make $25,000 between each of them. Black Gangster takes this deal and in response D-money hands him a Colt M1911 pistol.

Black Gangster shows up to the alleyway at midnight but doesn’t see anybody, just then D-money shows up with the buyer Amon Schwartz. D-money apologizes for being late and introduces Black Gangster to Amon. Amon in response says, “HERE’S THE MONEY, JUST GIMME THE WEED!” D-money then asks to see the money first, Amon shows it and the two do the deal. After getting the marijuana, Amon asks who this “Bald loser” (Black Gangster) is. Black Gangster in response threatens him with his gun. Amon runs off scared after this. The game ends with Black Gangster going back to the Mcdonald’s packing up getting ready to buy a house. This is where ‘Prologue PART 1’ ends.

The Ban of GangsterLifeLoverr4

The creator of the Remake series GangsterLifeLoverr4 was permanently banned from Scratch just 4 days after the release of his final episode. Just a few days before his ban he publicly revealed the development of D- moneys days, a prequel series to his Gangster Life Remakes. He was banned due to fans posting his projects to promotion studios, a moderator named PickledStudio deemed the entire series as too violent and decided to report Gangster Life: Story mode Remake Prologue PART 1. Six hours after this report was sent, the entire account along with all of the projects made under it were deleted. This occurred at 6:11 PM CST on the night of December 18th, 2023.
