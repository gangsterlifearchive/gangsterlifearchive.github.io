The fourth game and first official epilogue in the Gangster Life Remake series titled, “Gangster Life: Story mode Remake EPISODE 1 EPILOGUE” released on October 5th, 2023. When the game was first released, it was the shortest in the series, but later updates added more length, and an entirely new character to the series. This is the version presented here, as the updates were after November 1st, 2023. The thumbnail shows the later updates, which are now lost.

The epilogue starts with Black Gangster standing in front of a judge, he now has a prison jumpsuit with the numbers 1341 on the side of it. The police officer who arrested Black Gangster is also guarding the courtroom. The judge finds Black Gangster guilty for the murder of Amon Schwartz. This scene reveals Black Gangster's real name, Brandon Garrick. Black Gangster, angry at his sentence, and other minor inconveniences, then has a short conversation with his cellmate, Dan. Dan tells Black Gangster to calm down and in response Black Gangster screams at him. Dan then asks Black Gangster if he’d like to know what he was in for. In response Black Gangster says “NAWWWWWWWW!!!!! LET’S JUST SIT IN SILENCE!!!!” After this ‘EPISODE 1 EPILOGUE’ ends.
