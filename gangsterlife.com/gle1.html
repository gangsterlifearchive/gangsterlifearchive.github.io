The third game and first official episode in the Gangster Life Remake series, “Gangster Life: Story mode Remake EPISODE 1” the first episode in the remake series released on September 30th, 2023. It was released as a sequel to the first two prologues. The game was updated many times since its release, mostly minor bugfix updates. Major updates include entirely new backgrounds, and updated scripts.

The episode begins in front of the corn palace, Black Gangster spots Amon Schwartz (The buyer from the Prologue PART 1) sitting on the corn palace sign. Amon sees Black Gangster and says to him, “HEY U DA DUDE WHO SOLD ME DA DOPE! DAT DOPE WAS GARBAGE!! IMA KILL U” Black Gangster feels threatened by this, so he shoots Amon, killing him. A police officer then comes out of nowhere and tells Black Gangster that he’ll arrest him. Black Gangster doesn’t want to be arrested, so he says he didn’t do anything. The police officer, however, saw Black Gangster shoot Amon, so he gets arrested. The cop who arrests Black Gangster becomes the main antagonist after this. Black Gangster has his mugshot taken and after this ‘EPISODE 1” ends.
