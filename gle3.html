The seventh game and third episode in the series, “Gangster Life: Story mode Remake EPISODE 3” was released on November 1st, 2023. (the same day all the projects were archived) It was the first Gangster Life title to feature voice acting, it was also the first Gangster Life title to have official teasers and even a trailer.

The episode begins right where the second epilogue left off. Black Gangster gets ready to leave the pizza restaurant when he gets a call from D-money. Black Gangster thanks D-money a second time for giving him a pistol, D-money responds with “"AYE NO PROBLEM GANGSTA NO PROBLEM! But uhhh- u wanna know what I was gonna tell u?" Black Gangster then responds with “Yeah, what?” D-money then proceeds to explain that he set the two of them up with a drug deal. Black Gangster needs money after escaping from prison, so he agrees to go to the deal. Before hanging up the phone Black Gangster nervously asks “Holdup, what's da buyers name?” D-money then responds with “"Uhhhh, I think da dude said he was called Pete Daniels or sumin" This makes Black Gangster burst out laughing as he responds “LMAOO PETE DANIELS?! WHAT KINDA GOOFY NAME IS-” He’s interrupted by D-money who says “"Holdup Gangsta u can't be talkin, ain't yo real name Brandon Garrick?" Black Gangster then says “No- uhhh no, Who told u that?” D-money then explains "U was all over the news when u first got arrested, they had yo name everywhere Gangsta!" Black Gangster then says “Dam! I'm prolly America's most wanted rn D!” D-money then tells him “"Yup, das why u gonna need dis money Gangsta!"”

Black Gangster, hoping this deal is worth the money, asks D-money how much they’ll make. D-money tells him “"Bout 100k... EACH!" Black Gangster then asks “DAMMM! WHAT KINDA DOPE WE SELLIN FOR DAT MUCH?!?!?” D-money then explains “"Oh dis ain't no dope gangsta, were sellin dis dude CRYSTAL!" Black Gangster then eagerly says “BET! SEE U THERE AT 1!” He then hangs up the phone.
